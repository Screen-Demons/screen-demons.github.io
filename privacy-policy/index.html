Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Our Privacy Policy is critical to establishing and maintaining trust with you. At ScreenDemons, we believe you should know what data we collect, how we use it, and have meaningful control over both.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect and use your Personal Data when you use the ScreenDemons mobile application (the "Platform").

1. Apple Screen Time API & Family Controls

ScreenDemons relies heavily on Apple's Family Controls and Screen Time API (DeviceActivity) to provide its core functionality. Because this data is highly sensitive, we adhere to strict handling protocols:

  • No Granular Tracking: We do not track, collect, or transmit detailed, app-by-app usage history to our servers.
  • Metadata Only: The Platform only transmits aggregate metadata strictly necessary for gameplay (e.g., your total daily screen time, or a binary pass/fail signal triggered when a threshold is crossed locally on your device).
  • No Third-Party Selling: We never sell, barter, trade, or rent access to your screen time data, usage metadata, or Personal Data to advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties.

2. Personal Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, display name, and avatar image.
  • Authentication: We use secure OAuth providers (Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In). We do not store raw passwords.

Automatically Collected Information

We collect Personal Data automatically about how our users interact with the Platform, including IP addresses, device information, and time stamps. We also collect aggregate gameplay data, such as your ELO rating and challenge history.

3. How We Use Personal Data We Collect

We use the Personal Data collected in the following ways:

  • To present, operate, and improve the Platform.
  • To calculate ELO ratings, streaks, and challenge outcomes securely.
  • To display multiplayer leaderboards to your accepted friends.
  • To investigate or deter against fraudulent or illegal activity.

4. Sharing Information with Others

We may share your information with third-party vendors as necessary for them to perform their functions (e.g., secure database hosting via Supabase). These providers are required to keep your information confidential. We may also disclose data in response to a court order or to comply with a legal requirement.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have the right to request the complete deletion of your data at any time:

  • You can initiate account deletion directly from the Settings menu inside the Platform.
  • Upon requesting deletion, a 24-hour grace period begins. After this period, all Personal Data and challenge history are permanently purged from our servers.

6. Children

The Platform is not intended for or directed to children under the age of 13. We do not intentionally collect or maintain information about anyone under the age of 13.
